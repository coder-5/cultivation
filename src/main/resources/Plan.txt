1. CORE STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Major Ranks: 6 (Qi Gathering -> Immortality)
Minor Stages: 4 per Major Rank (Early, Middle, Late, Peak)
Substages: 10 per Minor Stage (G0, G1, G2, G3, G4, G5, G6, G7, G8, G9)
Total Steps: 240 Substages to Immortality

2. TALENT SYSTEM (Scale 0-21)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Formula: neededEXPToReachNextSubStage
-> (1 + currentSubStageIndex) * (22 - talent)

Formula: CultivationGainSpeed (Per Tick)
-> Random.nextFloat(0, (neededEXPToReachNextSubStage / (22 - talent)))

4. ABOLISHMENT & REBIRTH (The Talent Loop)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trigger: When current rank == Talent Ceiling (at G9).
Effect: Abolishing cultivation base.
- Current Cultivation Rank resets to Mortal (G0).
- Talent Score increases by +1 permanently.
- Combat Rank is preserved (does not reset).
- Player re-rolls for Sect Skip based on new higher Talent.

5. COMBAT RANK SYSTEM (10 Stages)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Each stage has 4 substages: Lesser, Normal, Greater, Peak.
1.  Martial Disciple
2.  Martial Master
3.  Martial Grandmaster
4.  Martial Lord
5.  Martial King
6.  Martial Emperor
7.  Martial Sovereign
8.  Martial Spirit
9.  Martial Ancestor
10. Martial Saint

6. ADVANCEMENT LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Substage G0 to G9: Accumulate EXP using formulas.
Minor Stage Breakthrough: At G9, trigger Heavenly Tribulation.
- Success: Advance to next stage G0.
- Failure: Potential health/Qi loss, stay at G9.
