WHAT IS IT: It is a Cultivation game.

INTRO: It will start by explaining what cultivation is and how it works.

RANKS: Each rank will be split into 4 minor stages, which will be each split into 10 substages.
The ranks are the following: Qi Gathering, Foundation Establishment, Core Formation, Nascent Soul, Deity Transformation, and immortality.

each major rank is split into early, middle, late, and peak stages.

each minor rank is split into the substages G0, G1, G2, G3, G4, G5, G6, G7, G8, G9.

Sonmeone must pass a heavenly tribulation to ascend a minor rank.

COMBAT: Players may battle to increase their combat rank, which follows similar principles to cultivation ranks,
but has 10 major stages and the same number of minors and substages.

The major Stages are: Martial Disciple, Martial Master, Martial Grandmaster, Martial Lord, Martial King, Martial Emperor, Martial Sovereign, Martial Spirit,
Martial Ancestor, and Martial Saint.

Each major stage has four substages: lesser, normal, greater, peak.

Sect: Users may join sects to gain contribution points which they may spend for pills.

Your rank deterimeies what tasks you can do and your discount on pills and other resources.

While your talent determines your starting rank, you must take a trial to rank up.

TALENT: Talent is a fundamental part of cultivation, with the following dependent on it:
    1. Starting rank in sect:
        A. Someone with potential to reach golden core will start as an outer disciple.
        B. Someone with potential to reach nascent soul will start as an inner disciple.
        C. Someone with potential to reach deity transformation will start as a core disciple.
        D. someone with potential to reach immortality will start as a chosen disciple.
        E. Someone Destined to remain mortal will start as a servant.
        F. Everyone else starts as a servant disciple disciple.
    2. Amount of qi needed to reach the next stage.
    3. maximum attainable cultivation rank. talent is on a scale of 0 to 16.
